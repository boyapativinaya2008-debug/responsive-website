Superstar Mahesh Babu

Mahesh Babu is one of the most celebrated actors in Telugu cinema, admired for his stylish screen presence and powerful performances.

Born on August 9, 1975, Mahesh Babu is known as the "Prince of Tollywood". He started his acting career as a child artist in Telugu films and later became a leading actor known for his versatility, charisma, and dedication to cinema. He has received several awards including Nandi Awards and Filmfare Awards South. Apart from movies, he is also involved in philanthropy, supporting education and healthcare initiatives. He is married to Namrata Shirodkar and has two children. His fan base spans across India and abroad, making him one of the most influential celebrities in Telugu cinema.
